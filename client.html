Troubleshooting FAQ

Quick tutorials for managing your connection status.

LOS is blinking RED

This means a fiber cable issue or terminal signal loss. Check if your cable drop line is bent, pinched, or disconnected.

LOS LED
Animated Alert: If blinking red persists, click Request Support Ticket.

Connected but No Internet

Power cycling clears router cache blocks. Unplug your adapter configuration from the outlet, wait 30 seconds, then replug.

30s Cycle Step
Safe Reboot: Allows terminal connection logs to re-authenticate from the server nodes.

Slow Internet Speed

Ensure no background network streams or updates are bottlenecking your data pool. Try running line tests near your terminal equipment.

Optimal Range: Wired LAN connections always achieve higher metrics than wireless radio paths.

Should I press the RESET button?

NEVER press the physical reset pinhole on the back plate. This clears your configuration scripts and permanently takes your device offline.

CRITICAL RESTRICTION: Pin resets require manual technician dispatch to restore.
