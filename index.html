Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you get connected faster.

What is the Advance Points system and how do I redeem rewards?
If you love planning ahead, this is for you! When you pay your bill *before* your billing cycle drops, you earn Advance Points. These points accumulate in your client portal and can be converted into awesome rewards like temporary speed boosts, custom N'RTECH merch, or even a completely free month of internet!
What are On-Time Points and how do I unlock secret discounts?
We reward consistency! Settling your bill exactly on or before your due date (the 20th of the month) automatically earns you On-Time Points. Accumulating these points unlocks our exclusive Secret Discounts—giving you surprise price cuts on your monthly subscription or heavy discounts on hardware upgrades like our Mini UPS.
How long does the installation process take?
Once your application is submitted and approved, standard installation takes between 1 to 3 business days, depending on your location and weather conditions.
When is my monthly due date?
Your official monthly billing cycle will always start on the 20th of the month. Any connection time before your first 20th is free of charge!
Do you have data caps or throttling?
No. All of our plans come with purely Unlimited Data. You can stream, download, and game as much as you want without worrying about hidden caps.
Do I need to buy my own router?
No need! The basic Modem/Wi-Fi Router is already included in your initial installation package. If you have a large house, we can assist you in adding extra routers or mesh networks for a fee.
What happens to my connection during brownouts?
Since we use purely fiber optic technology, our main lines remain active even during local grid power outages. As long as you have a backup power source (like a Mini UPS, power bank, or generator) to power your home router, your internet will stay connected!
